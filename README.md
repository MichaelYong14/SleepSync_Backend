# Sleep Sync: Your Sleeping Companion

**Project Description:**

Sleep Sync is a sleep management application designed to help users improve their sleep quality through personalized sleep schedules, sleep tracking, and more. With features like a smart alarm, relaxation routines, and progress tracking, Sleep Sync is your ultimate sleep companion.

---

## Features

- **Login Page and Menu**: Secure login system and user-friendly navigation menu.
- **Personalize Sleep Schedule**: Create a custom sleep schedule based on your needs.
- **Sleep Tracking and Analysis**: Track your sleep patterns and receive detailed insights and analysis.
- **Smart Alarm**: Wake up at the optimal time based on your sleep cycle.
- **Relaxation and Wind-Down Routine**: Engage in activities to help you relax before sleep.
- **Sleep Goal Setting and Progress**: Set sleep goals and track your progress over time.
